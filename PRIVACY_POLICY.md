# Privacy Policy for Memora (Re-Moment)

**Effective Date:** May 17, 2026

Welcome to Memora ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data is handled securely. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Memora mobile application (the "App").

## 1. Local Processing and Data Storage

Memora is designed with privacy at its core. By default:
- **On-Device Processing:** All basic indexing, photo analysis, and Vision AI processes happen directly on your device using Apple's local machine learning frameworks (e.g., CoreML, Vision, MLX). 
- **Local Data Storage:** Your photos, metadata, and generated stories are stored locally on your device or synced securely through your personal Apple iCloud account if enabled.
- **No Direct Access:** We do not have access to your personal photos, the metadata generated from them, or your iCloud data.

## 2. Cloud-based Pro Vision AI

If you choose to purchase and use the "Pro Vision AI" feature for enhanced photo captioning:
- **Image Transmission:** Selected images are temporarily transmitted to our secure servers to be processed by an advanced Vision-Language Model (VLM).
- **Data Retention Policy:** The images, generated captions, and any associated metadata sent to our servers are processed entirely in memory and are **deleted immediately** after the response is returned to your device. We do not store, log, or train on your photos.

## 3. Usage Data and Diagnostics

To improve Memora's stability and performance, we may collect anonymous crash reports and diagnostics using standard Apple frameworks (e.g., MetricKit). This data is completely anonymized and does not contain any personal photos or identifiable information.

## 4. In-App Purchases (IAP)

We offer in-app purchases via the Apple App Store. Apple handles the payment processing. We only receive anonymous receipt data required to validate your purchase and manage your Pro Vision AI quota. We do not have access to your payment details (such as credit card information).

## 5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.

## 6. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact MFLab-AI (Sang Hun Kim) or open an issue on our public repository.
